Welcome!

Mujhub is a blogging site developed to help freshers and 2nd yearites of Manipal University Jaipur by inviting seniors and college alumni to share their valuable experiences and provide guidance to budding engineers of our college. You can share about your college journey, strategies to apply for internships, a roadmap for placement, higher education opportunities, corporate lifestyle, tips for cracking tech interviews, or just a simple motivating post. To make a post you have to first register yourself by providing basic info about yourself and setting a strong password. Once you have registered successfully, you can login into your account, as an 'Author' and can make a post under 'Add Post' in the 'Dashboard'. In the dashboard you can view your all posts and can edit them whenever you want (updated posts shall be displayed on the home page). You can also view your profile in the dashboard itself. Don't forget to logout while leaving.
